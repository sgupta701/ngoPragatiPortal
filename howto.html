NGO Sign Up / Sign In

  1. For Already Signed up NGOs:
    1. Click on the "Sign In" Link
    2. Provide the three fields: Pan number, Mobile, or Email in userid field
    3. Provide your password
    4. Access your registered details
    5. In case you forget the password, there is an option to Sign In using OTP, where OTP is sent to the registered mobile number (alternated mobile number).





  1. For New NGOs for Sign Up:

    STEP 1:

    1. Provide your NGO Name
    2. Provide the contact person's Email and Mobile Number.
    3. Enter the captcha displayed at the website
    4. To proceed to step 2, check the link sent on your mail or phone number. It will direct you to Step 2.

    STEP 2:

    1. Enter NGO Pan Number. It will be checked and only the valid PAN Number will be accepted.

    STEP 3:

    1. This step will allow you to create a password
    2. Sign In to the portal with your credentials generated at the above steps.
